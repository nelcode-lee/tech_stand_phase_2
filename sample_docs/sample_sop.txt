SOP-001: Hand Washing Procedure

Scope
This procedure applies to all production areas at Cranswick sites.

Responsibilities
- Site Managers: ensure compliance
- Operatives: follow the procedure

Procedure
1. Wet hands under running water.
2. Apply soap and scrub for at least 20 seconds.
3. Rinse thoroughly under clean water.
4. Dry hands with disposable paper towels.
5. Use hand sanitiser before entering production area.

Records
Complete the Hand Washing Log after each wash.
